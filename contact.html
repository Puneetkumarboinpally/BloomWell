✦ Before you write

Frequently asked questions

Can I submit an article or guest post?

+

Yes — we accept pitches from qualified writers, healthcare professionals, and researchers. We don't publish generic wellness content; we're looking for pieces grounded in evidence and written with genuine expertise. Read our contributor guidelines for more detail, then use the contact form above with "Writer pitch" selected.

Do you accept sponsored content or paid placements?

+

No. We do not publish sponsored articles, accept payment to recommend products, or allow brands to influence our editorial content. This is a firm policy. We do accept clearly-labelled sponsorships for the newsletter and occasional event partnerships — but these are always disclosed and never affect our editorial independence.

I'm having trouble with my newsletter subscription.

+

Check your spam or promotions folder first — our emails sometimes land there on the first send. If you need to change your email address or update your preferences, click "Manage preferences" at the bottom of any newsletter email. Still stuck? Use the form above and select "Newsletter help".

Can I republish or quote your articles?

+

Short quotes with attribution are generally fine for editorial and non-commercial purposes. For anything more substantial — reprinting full articles, commercial use, or translation — please get in touch via the contact form. We're usually happy to work something out.

Do you offer personalised health advice?

+

No. BloomWell publishes general health information and education — not personal medical advice. Nothing on our site should be taken as a substitute for advice from your own GP, dietitian, or other qualified healthcare professional. If you have a specific health concern, please speak with a qualified practitioner.
